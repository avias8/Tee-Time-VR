Introduction

"Tee Time VR" is developed and published by Avi Varma, a solo developer doing business as Fiskar Varmations ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we process when you play Tee Time VR, why we process it, and the choices you have regarding that information. Meta may ask you to review this policy before granting access to certain platform features, such as multiplayer services.

Information We Process

We process only the information that is necessary to operate the game, deliver platform features, and provide support. We do not sell your information or use it for advertising.

  • Meta account information. When you sign in through the Meta platform, we receive your Meta (Oculus) user ID, display name, and profile image. We use this information to authenticate you, show your profile to other players in lobbies and leaderboards, and to enable friend invites and matchmaking provided by the Meta platform.
  • Gameplay and progression data. We store your local player preferences, achievements, and leaderboard scores so the game can save your progress, present rankings, and prevent cheating. When you participate in multiplayer matches, your shot data and voice-chat participation are transmitted in real time to the other players in the session.
  • Support information. If you contact us at privacy@avivarma.ca we will process the email address, message contents, and any attachments you choose to send so that we can respond to your request.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Create and maintain multiplayer lobbies, leaderboards, and other social gameplay features;
  • Synchronize gameplay data across your devices when you use your Meta account;
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests;
  • Monitor performance, detect bugs, and protect the Service against abuse.

Legal Basis

We process your information because it is necessary to perform the contract with you (the End User License Agreement governing access to Tee Time VR), to provide the platform features you request, and to comply with our legitimate interests in operating a secure and fair game environment. Data Storage and Retention

Your profile, leaderboard scores, and gameplay history are stored securely on Meta-hosted services and within your device storage. We retain this information while you maintain an active Tee Time VR profile. Diagnostic logs are retained for up to 12 months unless we need them longer to investigate an issue. Support correspondence is retained for as long as necessary to resolve your request and to comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with independent third parties. Meta Platforms, Inc. processes the data described above as part of providing platform services such as account authentication, matchmaking, and cloud storage. Multiplayer participants can see your display name, avatar, voice chat audio (if enabled), and gameplay actions during a session.

Your Rights and Choices

All players, regardless of where they live, may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information. To exercise these rights, email us at the address below with your Meta display name or user ID so that we can verify your account. You can also review or update certain account details directly through your Meta account settings. When you ask us to delete your Tee Time VR profile, we will coordinate with Meta to remove stored data associated with your user ID and confirm once the deletion is complete. We do not charge any fees for handling privacy requests.

Children's Privacy

Tee Time VR is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly process personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated policy here and revise the effective date. Continued use of Tee Time VR after the changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.
