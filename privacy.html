Introduction

"Tee Time VR" is developed and published by Avi Varma ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we process when you play Tee Time VR, why we process it, and the choices you have regarding that information. Meta may ask you to review this policy before granting access to certain platform features, such as multiplayer services.

Information We Process

We process only the information that is necessary to operate the game, deliver platform features, and provide support. We do not sell your information or use it for advertising.

  • Meta account information. When you sign in through the Meta platform, we receive your Meta (Oculus) user ID, display name, and profile image. We use this information to authenticate you, show your profile to other players in lobbies and leaderboards, and to enable friend invites and matchmaking provided by the Meta platform.
  • Gameplay and progression data. We store your local player profile, current handicap, match history, achievements, and leaderboard scores so the game can save your progress, present rankings, and prevent cheating. When you participate in multiplayer matches, your shot data and voice-chat participation are transmitted in real time to the other players in the session.
  • Diagnostics and device data. We process your IP address, approximate region, device model, system language, and crash logs that are provided automatically by the Meta platform. This information allows us to choose the best-performing regional server, troubleshoot technical issues, and improve performance.
  • Support information. If you contact us at HarshvardhanV98@gmail.com we will process the email address, message contents, and any attachments you choose to send so that we can respond to your request.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Verify that you own the app and are entitled to access multiplayer features;
  • Create and maintain multiplayer lobbies, leaderboards, and other social gameplay features;
  • Synchronize gameplay data across your devices when you use your Meta account;
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests;
  • Monitor performance, detect bugs, and protect the Service against abuse.

Data Storage and Retention

Your profile, leaderboard scores, and gameplay history are stored securely on Meta-hosted services and within your device storage. We retain this information while you maintain an active Tee Time VR profile. Diagnostic logs are retained for up to 12 months unless we need them longer to investigate an issue. Support correspondence is retained for as long as necessary to resolve your request and to comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with independent third parties. Meta Platforms, Inc. processes the data described above as part of providing platform services such as account authentication, matchmaking, and cloud storage. Multiplayer participants can see your display name, avatar, voice chat audio (if enabled), and gameplay actions during a session.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information. You can exercise these rights by contacting us at the email address listed below. You can also review or update certain account details directly through your Meta account settings. If you wish to delete your Tee Time VR profile, contact us and we will work with Meta to remove stored data associated with your user ID.

Tee Time VR is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly process personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.
